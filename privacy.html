Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2026

Thank you for using Travel Documents Organizer.

Your privacy is important to us. This app is designed with a strong focus on privacy and security.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data on external servers.

All data you add to the app, including documents, file names, notes, and attached images, is stored locally on your device only.

2. Permissions We Use

Photo Library Access

This app requires access to your photo library to allow you to attach images to your documents. We only access the photos you choose and do not scan or collect your entire photo library.

Face ID / Passcode

Face ID (or device passcode) is used to securely lock and protect your documents within the app. Your biometric data is handled by Apple and is never accessed or stored by us.

File Access

This app allows you to import and manage documents from your device using the system file picker. We only access files you explicitly select.

3. Data Sharing

We do not:

  • Sell your data
  • Share your data
  • Transfer your data to third parties

4. Data Security

All your data remains on your device. We do not use any external servers, cloud storage, or remote databases.

5. Third-Party Services

This app does not use:

  • Analytics tools
  • Tracking services
  • Advertising networks

6. Children’s Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.
